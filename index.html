The Catalog Shopper?

We supply high quality corporate
and premium gifts, including housewares to
major corporations and retail outfits worldwide. The company has been in business of importing,
wholesaling, and retailing since 1994.

What makes us so special?

We can make whatever our clients require. Products include a wide array of item that range from important everyday products such as umbrellas, towels and lights to premium items like accessories, porcelain and electronics.
